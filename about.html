About Me

Who am I?

My name is Jackson Crittenden.

I am a technical artist and designer from Toronto. I studied Digital Futures and Integrated Media at OCAD University. During my time at OCAD, I learned various skills in both digital and physical realms. I am a bit of a generalist. I love problem-solving.

The most important part of my work is my process. I work in sprints so I can iterate as much as possible. I tend to do mini-critiques throughout the project timeline to get lots of feedback. I find that more iterations and lots of feedback leads to stronger work.
